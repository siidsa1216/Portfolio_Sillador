Likha Filipino

Likha Filipino is a mobile application designed to empower and connect the Filipino artistic community. It serves as a vibrant online platform where Filipino artists can showcase their diverse talents in various art forms, from visual arts and music to writing and photography. The app fosters a space for collaboration and interaction, allowing artists to network with each other, share their creative journeys, and provide valuable feedback.

Likha Filipino is more than just an app; it's a virtual hub that cultivates creativity, fosters connections, and celebrates the vibrant spirit of Filipino art and culture.
